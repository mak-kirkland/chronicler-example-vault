The **Adoran Family** are a ||secretive family of assassins|| (click to reveal spoiler). 

Their motto is <span style="font-family:'Uncial Antiqua'; color:#ff113e; font-size:1.5rem">"Shadow Binds Us"</span>.


## History

Founded approximately 2500 years ago by [[Nocturnus]], the first of the Shadow Monks. With his wife Dante, they established themselves as the deadliest assassins in the world.
