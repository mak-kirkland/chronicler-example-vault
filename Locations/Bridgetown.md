**Bridgetown** is the primary trade hub of the Ocla region. Built over the ruins of an ancient elven aqueduct, the city spans both sides of the River Serpentine. It is a place of immense wealth and crushing poverty.

The **Old District** (pictured left) remains the safest part of the city. The architecture here dates back to the **Second Era**, characterized by high stone arches and slate roofs. 

While the [[City Guard]] patrols these streets heavily, the sewers beneath belong to the **Rat King**. Entrance to the sewers is strictly forbidden by penalty of death (or worse).
