The **Kow'Errata Spiralis** is a galaxy located in the Virgo Supercluster. It is currently in a state of cold war between the **Technocracy** and the **Free Systems Alliance**.

### Political Situation

The capital world, **Onidepix**, serves as the diplomatic neutral ground.

* **The Technocracy**: Controls the core. <img src="Antunaca Map (Sigma).png" style="height: 1.5em; vertical-align: middle; border-radius: 50%;"> (Symbol)
* **Free Systems**: Controls the outer rim. <img src="Ira Mundi Map (Sigma).jpg" style="height: 1.5em; vertical-align: middle; border-radius: 50%;"> (Symbol)
