**Castor** is a mercenary currently operating out of [[Bridgetown]]. Known for his quick wit and quicker daggers, he claims to have once been a noble of the **Onidepix** courts before a "minor misunderstanding" involving the royal treasury.

He is currently traveling with a party of adventurers.
